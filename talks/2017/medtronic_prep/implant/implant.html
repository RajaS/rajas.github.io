Axillary vein

Axillary vein puncture

  • Fluoro guided
  • Junction of clavicle and first rib
  • Walk along first rib

Axillary vein - pros and cons

  • ( - ) Small learning curve
  • ( - ) Needs fluoroscopy
  • ( - ) Needs venogram (myth !)
  • ( + ) Very low risk of pneumothorax
  • ( + ) No lead crush
